TERRA BAD WEATHER

WHY IS THIS NEEDED?

In every war, there is a third force that has no flags and knows no allies—the Earth itself. We use data from NASA’s Terra satellite to show how natural anomalies and phenomena have become silent participants in the war in Ukraine, changing the course of history before our eyes.

Terra satellite data is a vast array of information, incomprehensible to most people. Our task is to transform this complex data into understandable and clear visualizations.

We strive to make science accessible and relevant by turning numbers and graphs into a compelling story that everyone can understand.



A LITTLE ABOUT US

WireOnFire is a team of enthusiasts who combine science, technology, and art to tell stories about our planet. We create interactive web resources and animations that show the impact of natural processes and environmental changes on modern life.

Our project examines how natural anomalies — fires, droughts, frosts, dust storms, and others — affect the conduct of military operations, particularly in Ukraine. Using data from NASA's Terra satellite, we demonstrate how climate change is altering the tactics, logistics, and safety of military operations.

We aim to not only inform, but also inspire — to help people better understand the Earth so they can protect it.
