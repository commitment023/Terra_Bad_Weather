People competing to share weather-related events using Terra

The WireOnFire team is a group of enthusiasts who have combined science, technology, and art to tell important stories about our planet. We specialize in creating interactive web resources and animation projects that demonstrate the impact of natural processes and environmental changes on modern life. Our current project is dedicated to studying the relationship between natural anomalies and warfare, in particular in Ukraine. Using data from the Terra satellite, we show how climate change, fires, droughts, frosts, dust storms, and other natural phenomena affect the tactics, logistics, and safety of military operations. The goal of WireOnFire is not only to inform, but also to inspire people to a deeper understanding of the planet that we all have to protect. Our approach combines: Scientific accuracy - real NASA Terra satellite data; Visual effects and animation - interactive graphics and dynamic maps; Stories of people and nature - how environmental change affects life and safety. WireOnFire believes that data can tell stories as powerfully as words or pictures, and that it is through them that we can better understand the world and avoid disasters.
